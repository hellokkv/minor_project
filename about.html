Who We Are

inkribeAI is a modern SaaS platform that allows you to build and share intelligent, responsive forms like never before. Whether you're creating applications, surveys, or lead capture forms — we make it effortless with drag-and-drop tools, voice input, and stylus writing.

Our Core Features

Voice Input

Fill forms using just your voice. Fast, hands-free, and fully accessible for all users.

Stylus Support

Enable handwritten answers and signatures using stylus or touchscreens.

Instant Templates

Start with beautiful, ready-made templates for surveys, registration, feedback, and more.

Real-time Sync

Save all your data instantly with our Firebase cloud backend for seamless access.
